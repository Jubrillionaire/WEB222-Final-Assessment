Directions to the Hidden Gem

Walking

From the city center, head east on Main Street. Turn left on Park Avenue and continue for two blocks.

Driving

Take Highway 101 and exit at Oak Street. Follow the signs to the Hidden Gem parking lot.

Transit

Take Bus Route 22 and get off at the Hidden Gem stop. It's a short walk from there.

What to Do/Eat/See

Activities

Explore the beautiful trails, have a picnic by the lake, and enjoy birdwatching.

Food

There's a charming cafe that serves delicious sandwiches and freshly brewed coffee.

Sights

Don't miss the breathtaking view from the hilltop viewpoint overlooking the city.
