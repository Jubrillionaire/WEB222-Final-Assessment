About Me

Meet the Creator

I'm Jubril Akolade, a computer programming student of Seneca College.

Throughout my life, I've been drawn to lesser-known wonders. My curiosity has led me to investigate little-known stories and stories that deserve attention.

The inspiration for this platform came from my personal experiences of coming across breathtaking art pieces, groundbreaking scientific theories, captivating historical anecdotes, and cultural nuances that did not receive the attention that they truly deserved. There are a number of incredible accomplishments and creations that are obscured by the overwhelming noise of mainstream media. These hidden gems carry the potential to spark inspiration, broaden perspectives, and offer a fresh lens through which to view the world.
